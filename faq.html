FAQ

What is this app?

This app helps tourists and guests to navigate and plan olympic games. It also provides translation features.

How do you change the language preference?

Click navigation button on the top-left corner -> Settings -> Language -> Choose your language.

How to use the translation feature?

Translation button -> Choose the language you want to change to -> Type or click the mic in the keyboard to say what you want to translate. -> Click translate button
